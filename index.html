What is Metaverse

Metaverse is an open-source public blockchain that aims to provide asset digitization and identity capabilities. Through Blockchain-as-a-Service (BaaS), we make convenient, secure digital finance services accessible to all Internet applications at virtually no cost.

Founded in 2016, Metaverse went live in February of 2017. We strongly believe that blockchain technology is the catalyst for a better future - the New Reality.

The New Reality

Interested to learn more? Please visit MVS Technical Features

Digital Assets Digital assets on the Metaverse blockchain are trust-free, traceable and immutable decentralized virtual assets that can be freely registered, transferred, issued, used as collateral and burnt.
Digital Identity Digital identities are self-sovereign identity ledgers on the Metaverse blockchain. Digital credit is created through interactions with assets and other identities.
Oracle Intermediaries Oracles are service providers with digital identities. Rather than decentralization, Metaverse integrates intermediaries into the blockchain, providing rapid and transparent supervision.
Decentralized Exchange Metaverse allows value to be exchanged freely and easily. Control of assets is returned to the user, preventing asset theft.

ETP Markets

Metaverse ETP is the token used on Metaverse and can be used to measure the value of smart properties in Metaverse or as collateral in financial transactions. ETP is also used to pay transaction and other fees applied on Metaverse.
